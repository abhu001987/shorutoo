Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

At Shoruto, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account info: Email address and username (if you create an account).
  • Usage data: Test scores, answer content, and progress — all stored securely to help you track improvement.
  • Technical data: Browser type, IP address (anonymized), and device info to optimize performance.

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us deliver a personalized learning experience. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Generate mock tests and evaluate answers using AI (your answer content is never shared).
  • Provide feedback and suggestions to improve your performance.
  • Send occasional service updates (you can opt out at any time).

🔒 We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose. Your privacy is paramount.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use analytics or tracking cookies that follow you across the web. Our cookie usage is minimal and transparent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectify — correct any inaccurate information.
  • Erase — ask us to delete your account and associated data.
  • Object — opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at privacy@shoruto.com. We respond within 72 hours.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Test performance data is anonymized and retained for aggregate analytics (no individual identification).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we'll notify you via email or through the platform. Continued use of Shoruto signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Questions? If you have any concerns about your privacy, please reach out to privacy@shoruto.com. We're here to help.

This privacy policy is part of our commitment to transparency and trust. Thank you for using Shoruto.
