About Shoruto

Every subject, understood — not just memorized.

Shoruto started as a simple frustration: the best explanation of a topic was always scattered across ten different sources — a textbook paragraph here, a YouTube comment there, a half-remembered lecture slide somewhere else. Nobody had put it together in one place, in a way that actually made sense.

So we built the place. Shoruto pulls together trending topics, structured subjects, and AI-assisted breakdowns so you spend your time understanding — not hunting.

What we believe

Three ideas everything we build runs through.

Clarity over clutter

If an explanation needs a glossary to understand the glossary, we've failed. Every summary is written to be understood on the first read.

Respect your time

Trending topics, subject breakdowns, and AI answers live in one place so you spend minutes finding what you need, not hours.

Built with students

Every feature on Shoruto started as feedback from someone actually studying with it. We read every message.
