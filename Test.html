Explain · PM E-DRIVE

PM E-DRIVE (Electric Drive Revolution in Innovative Vehicle Enhancement) is a central sector scheme launched to accelerate EV adoption in India.

Key features:

  • Upfront incentives for 2W, 3W, e-buses, and e-trucks
  • Allocates funds for charging infrastructure
  • Supports advanced cell chemistry battery manufacturing
  • Promotes R&D in battery technology

Significance:

  • Reduces oil imports and fossil fuel dependence
  • Lowers carbon emissions and urban pollution
  • Fosters a domestic EV manufacturing hub
  • Creates jobs in the EV ecosystem

The scheme is a key part of India's transition to clean mobility and aligns with the country's climate commitments.
